Home Buying Process<\/h1>\n\n\n\n
\n
start your home buying search<\/a><\/div>\n<\/div>\n<\/div><\/div>\n\n\n\n
\n
\n

The home buying process all starts with the right real estate agent. Choosing the right one is an important step in buying a home because this is the person that will guide you throughout the entire buying process. The best agent will be honest with you and won\u2019t try to push you into buying a house you aren\u2019t sure about.<\/p>\n<\/div>\n<\/div>\n\n\n\n

\n

How It Works<\/h2>\n\n\n\n
\n
\n

Educate Yourself.<\/strong><\/h3>\n\n\n\n

Begin by doing some online research to familiarize yourself with some of your options when buying a home. Start thinking about what town you want to live in and how much you are comfortable spending on a home. <\/p>\n<\/div>\n\n\n\n

\n

Pre-Approval Process.<\/strong><\/h3>\n\n\n\n

Getting pre-approved for a loan allows you to see how much a bank will actually lend you to purchase a home. This way you won\u2019t waste time looking at homes priced out of your range.<\/p>\n<\/div>\n\n\n\n

\n

Property Search.<\/strong><\/h3>\n\n\n\n

This is the fun part! Our unique computerized buyer Buyer Advantage Program allows you to simply enter towns you\u2019re interested in, price range, style of home and other particulars that are important to you. This system also lets you know about new listings as soon as they hit the market!<\/p>\n<\/div>\n\n\n\n

\n

Make an Offer.<\/h3>\n\n\n\n

Once you find the perfect home we will submit a written offer on the home. Keep in mind that contract negotiations are not limited to price, they also include timing, contingencies, and much more. <\/p>\n<\/div>\n\n\n\n

\n

Choose an Attorney.<\/strong><\/h3>\n\n\n\n

The reason for hiring your attorney now is to get them in contact with the seller\u2019s attorney to draft the Purchase and Sales Agreement. A good attorney will protect the buyer throughout the transaction.<\/p>\n<\/div>\n\n\n\n

\n

Home Inspection.<\/strong><\/h3>\n\n\n\n

Now that you have secured a property, it is time to schedule the appropriate inspections on the property. We highly recommend buyers spend the money on a home inspection. The report alone is very much worth the cost. goes here<\/p>\n<\/div>\n\n\n\n

\n

Purchase & Sale.<\/strong><\/h3>\n\n\n\n

Now that the inspections are complete, a purchase and sale agreement needs to be prepared by the attorneys and both buyer and seller must sign. The P&S agreement gives more detail of the transaction.<\/p>\n<\/div>\n\n\n\n

\n

Closing.<\/strong><\/h3>\n\n\n\n

During the actual closing, deeds, loan papers, and other documents are prepared, signed and filed with the registry. At this point, congratulations will be in order now that you are a homeowner!<\/p>\n<\/div>\n<\/div>\n<\/div>\n\n\n\n
